## 1. Introduction

Keto ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Business Data**: Product inventory, sales records, expenses, and financial information you manually enter
- **Shop Information**: Shop name and settings you configure
- **Account Settings**: Language preferences, theme preferences, currency selection, notification settings

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Analytics**: App usage patterns, feature interactions (via Firebase Analytics)
- **Crash Reports**: Error logs and crash information (via Firebase Crashlytics)
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, app version
- **Ad Interaction Data**: How you interact with advertisements (via Google Mobile Ads)

### 2.3 Local Storage
- All your business data is stored locally on your device's database
- We do NOT send your business data to external servers without your consent

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:
- **App Functionality**: Store and manage your business data, preferences, and settings
- **Improvement**: Analyzing app performance and user behavior to improve features
- **Error Tracking**: Identifying and fixing bugs through crash reports
- **Monetization**: Displaying relevant advertisements to support app development
- **Notifications**: Sending you alerts about low stock levels (with your permission)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Your business data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- **Firebase Services**: We use Firebase to collect analytics and crash reports
- **Encryption**: Sensitive data is stored securely on your device
- **No Selling**: We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

### 5.1 Google Mobile Ads
- Displays advertisements in the app
- Collects ad interaction data for personalization
- See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 5.2 Firebase (Google)
- **Firebase Analytics**: Tracks app usage patterns
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: Collects crash reports for debugging
- See [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

### 5.3 Google Play Services
- Required for app functionality and updates
- See [Google Play Services Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 5.4 Image Picker & Camera
- Requires device permission to access photos
- Photos are processed locally on your device only

### 5.5 Notifications
- Requires permission to send alerts
- Used only for low stock warnings you've enabled

## 6. Permissions Explained

The App requests the following permissions:
- **Camera/Photos**: To add product images (optional)
- **Notifications**: To alert you about low stock levels (optional)
- **Storage**: To save and access your business database
- **Internet**: To display ads and send crash reports

## 7. Data Export and Deletion

- **Export**: You can export your business data from the app settings
- **Deletion**: You can permanently delete all your data by uninstalling the app
- **Local Control**: You have complete control over your data on your device

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information immediately.

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access and review your personal data
- Export your business data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Disable notifications at any time
- Disable ads by removing ad permissions
- Opt-out of analytics (through app settings)

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your continued use of the App after any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
